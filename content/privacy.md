**Last updated:** March 9, 2026

At Dastkaari ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit **dastkaari.com**, how we use it, and your rights as a visitor.

## What Data We Collect

We do **not** require you to create an account, and we do **not** collect personal information through forms or registrations. Our data collection is limited to the analytics and advertising tools described below.

## Google Analytics (GA4)

We use Google Analytics 4 (measurement ID: **G-ZWG0FTJ2H1**) to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects:

- Pages visited, time on page, and navigation paths
- Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
- Device type, browser, and operating system
- Referral source (how you found us)

Google Analytics uses **anonymized IP addresses** — your full IP address is not stored. Data is processed by Google LLC in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Google AdSense

We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other websites. This includes the use of the **DoubleClick cookie**, which enables Google and its partners to serve personalized ads.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/) or the [Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/).

## Cookies

Our site uses cookies from the following sources:

- **Google Analytics** — to track anonymized usage data
- **Google AdSense** — to serve and measure advertisements
- **Cloudflare** — for security, performance optimization, and basic analytics (Cloudflare may set cookies such as `__cflb` and `__cf_bm` to manage traffic and protect against bots)

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on some parts of the site.

## Affiliate Links

Some of our content contains affiliate links to third-party services, including but not limited to **Wise, N26, Expatrio, Lingoda, GetSafe, ImmobilienScout24, and NordVPN**. When you click these links, the third-party site may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We earn a commission on qualifying purchases or sign-ups at no additional cost to you.

We only recommend products and services we believe are genuinely useful for our audience.

## No Personal Data Collection

As of the last updated date, we do not:

- Require user accounts or logins
- Collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers through forms
- Store any personal data on our servers

If this changes in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

## Your Rights Under GDPR

Since our site serves an audience in the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Right to access** — You can request information about what data, if any, we hold about you.
- **Right to erasure** — You can request deletion of any personal data we may hold.
- **Right to restriction** — You can request that we limit how your data is processed.
- **Right to object** — You can object to data processing, including opting out of analytics and personalized advertising.
- **Right to data portability** — You can request your data in a machine-readable format.

Since we do not collect personal data directly, most of these rights pertain to data collected by third-party tools (Google Analytics, Google AdSense, Cloudflare). Please refer to their respective privacy policies to exercise your rights with those services.

## How to Opt Out of Tracking

- **Google Analytics:** Install the [Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
- **Personalized ads:** Visit [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/) or [YourAdChoices](http://optout.aboutads.info/).
- **Cookies:** Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Most browsers allow you to manage cookies under "Privacy" or "Security" settings.
