## A Quick Note on Immigration Advice

While we do our best to provide accurate, well-researched guides about moving to and living in Germany, we are **not immigration lawyers or licensed consultants**. We cannot review individual cases, assess your visa eligibility, or provide personalized legal advice.

For case-specific guidance, we recommend consulting a qualified immigration lawyer (Rechtsanwalt fur Auslanderrecht) or contacting your local Auslanderbehorde.
