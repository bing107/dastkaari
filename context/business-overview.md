# PakGermany — Business Overview

## Status: Pre-launch

## Concept
Content-driven website helping Pakistanis move to Germany and build a life here. Monetized through ads, affiliates, paid guides, and consultancy.

## Founders
Berlin-based Pakistani entrepreneurs with 10+ years in Germany. Direct experience hiring and relocating talent from Pakistan.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Pakistani professionals (25-40) considering moving to Germany
- **Secondary:** Pakistani expats in Germany (settling in, investing, career growth)
- **Tertiary:** Pakistani students planning to study in Germany

## Competitive Advantage
- First-hand Pakistan → Germany experience (not generic expat content)
- Both immigration AND investment angles (unique combo)
- Can offer real consultancy backed by lived experience
- Underserved niche — most German expat content is Western-focused
